Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5/18/2026

Pinterest Management App ("the tool") is a personal-use application operated by Lance Kirby. This policy describes how data is handled.

Scope

The tool is a single-operator application. It is used exclusively by Lance Kirby to manage their own Etsy shop and Pinterest account. There are no external users, no user accounts beyond the operator's own, and no public-facing services.

Data accessed

Through authorized API connections, the tool accesses:

  • Etsy shop data belonging to the operator: listings, orders, shop statistics, and metadata
  • Pinterest account data belonging to the operator: pins, boards, and analytics

The tool only accesses data owned by the operator. It does not access data belonging to other users or customers beyond what is necessary to fulfill the operator's own orders.

How data is stored

All data is stored locally on the operator's own hardware. No data is shared with third parties, sold, or transmitted to external services other than the platform APIs from which it originates.

Customer data

Customer information visible through the Etsy orders API (such as buyer names and shipping addresses for the operator's own orders) is handled in accordance with Etsy's seller policies and applicable privacy law. This information is used solely to fulfill orders and is not shared, sold, or used for any other purpose.

Third-party APIs

The tool connects to Etsy and Pinterest APIs under the operator's own developer credentials, subject to those platforms' terms and privacy policies.

Data retention

Locally stored data is retained as needed for the operator's record-keeping and may be deleted at any time at the operator's discretion.
