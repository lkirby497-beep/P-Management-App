Pinterest Management App is a personal dashboard and workflow tool for managing a small digital products business. It connects to platforms like Etsy and Pinterest to help the operator track listings, monitor performance, schedule content, and manage product files in one place.

What it does

  • Aggregates sales and analytics data from Etsy and Pinterest into a single dashboard
  • Helps the operator schedule and organize Pinterest pins
  • Manages digital product files and listing metadata
  • Provides reporting on shop performance over time

Who it's for

This is a single-operator tool built and used by Lance Kirby to manage their own Etsy shop and Pinterest account. It is not a public service and does not have external users.
